Remote work and leave policy

Full-time engineers may work remotely up to four days per week. Core collaboration hours are 11:00 to 16:00 IST on weekdays. Managers may require in-office attendance for launch weeks and incident reviews.

Paid time off accrues at 1.5 days per month, capped at 18 days per calendar year. Unused PTO does not carry over unless approved in writing by People Ops before December 15.

Sick leave is separate and limited to 10 days per year. Absences longer than three consecutive workdays require a medical note. Parental leave is 16 weeks for primary caregivers and 4 weeks for secondary caregivers.

Expense reimbursements under 5000 INR can be self-approved in the finance portal. Amounts above 5000 INR require manager approval before purchase.
