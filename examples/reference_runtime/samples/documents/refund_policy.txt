Acme Retail refund policy

Customers may return unused products within 30 days of delivery for a full refund to the original payment method. Items must include original packaging and proof of purchase. Digital downloads and personalized goods are not eligible for return unless defective.

Shipping costs for returns are paid by the customer unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect. Damaged shipments must be reported within 7 days of delivery with photos attached to the support ticket.

Refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days after the warehouse confirms receipt of the returned item. Chargebacks filed before contacting support may pause the refund review.
